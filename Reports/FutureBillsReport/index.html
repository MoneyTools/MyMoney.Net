Future Bills Report#

This report tries to find recurring payments form transactions with a Category of type Expense or RecurringExpense so it can forecast what your upcoming payments might be over the next 12 months.

You might see something like this:

It will repeat this out for 12 months and then show the total for the year.

You can force a category top show up in this report by changing the type to RecurringExpense.
